# Refund Policy

## Rule 1: Refund Window

Refund requests must be made within 30 days of the delivery date. Products may
specify a shorter return window; the shorter window applies. Requests made after
the applicable window are denied.

## Rule 2: Opened Electronics

Opened electronics are non-refundable. If any electronics item in the order has
been opened, that item is not eligible for a refund. Unopened electronics in
original packaging remain eligible within the refund window.

## Rule 3: Refund Frequency Limit

A maximum of two refunds are allowed per customer within any rolling 12-month
period. Customers who have already received two refunds in the last 12 months
are denied further refunds.

## Rule 4: VIP Manual Override

VIP-tier customers are entitled to one manual override per calendar year. The
override may be used to approve a refund that would otherwise be denied by
Rule 1 or Rule 3, provided there is no fraud concern. The override cannot be
applied to opened electronics or digital products.

## Rule 5: High-Value Manual Review

Orders exceeding ₹50,000 in total value require manual review by a human agent
before any refund is issued. The agent must escalate; it must not approve or
deny these requests automatically.

## Rule 6: Digital Products

Digital products (software licenses, gift cards, downloadable content) are
non-refundable once delivered.

## Rule 7: Damaged Items

Items reported damaged on arrival are eligible for a refund within the refund
window regardless of opened status, subject to fraud checks and Rules 3 and 5.

## Rule 8: Fraud Check

Refunds are blocked for accounts flagged with a high fraud risk score. Such
requests must be escalated to manual review rather than approved or denied
automatically.
